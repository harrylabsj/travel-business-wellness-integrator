# Business Travel Wellness Integrator
Integrates wellness practices into business travel for sustained performance — from pre-trip preparation to post-trip recovery.
## What it does
- Performs a **travel wellness assessment** to identify health risks and gaps in current business travel routines
- Builds a **routine adaptation framework** for in-flight wellness, hotel-room workouts, jet-lag management, and nutrition
- Designs **recovery optimization** protocols including sleep banking, immune support, and post-trip decompression planning
## Example scenarios
**1. 出差当牛做马还要顾身体**
> 👤 我在上海上班，每周都要出差去深圳/北京/成都，不是在酒店就是在高铁飞机上。胃病犯了还不敢请假，有什么保命方案？
> 🤖 输出打工人出差保命手册——高铁场景（常温矿泉水+水果切、拒绝动车盒饭）、酒店场景（烧开水烫马桶圈和遥控器、随身折叠烧水壶）、吃饭场景（外卖点当地菜而非连锁快餐、每口咀嚼20下）、作息场景（23:30强制关机、全黑窗帘+蒸汽眼罩），一周最多3天出差。

**2. Fly 3x/month work travel wellness**
> 👤 I fly 3x/month for work and feel destroyed. Design a wellness integration system for my business travel.
> 🤖 End-to-end wellness system — pre-trip preparation (sleep banking, immune support), in-flight routine (hydration schedule, compression socks, movement), hotel-room workout (bodyweight 15-min circuit), jet-lag management (light-exposure schedule), nutrition strategy, and post-trip recovery protocol.

**3. Conference trip wellness plan**
> 👤 Turn my 5-day conference trip into something that doesn't wreck my health goals.
> 🤖 Conference wellness plan — schedule audit, networking nutrition strategy, sleep-protection tactics, exercise micro-dosing (10-min sessions between sessions), and alcohol-moderation scripts.
